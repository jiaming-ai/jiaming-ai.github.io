Looking for Student Researchers

I'm actively seeking motivated students to work on cutting-edge problems in robot navigation, SLAM, and vision-language models. My research has been published at top venues including ICRA 2024 (Best Paper Award), CoRL 2023, and IROS 2023, with code open-sourced and used by labs worldwide. I bring an entrepreneurial mindset to research—emphasizing rapid prototyping, real-world deployment, and practical impact.

Past students have gone on to exceptional opportunities: PhD programs at MIT, research positions at Google, and collaborations with top institutions. I believe in hands-on mentorship, giving students ownership of projects, and fostering both technical excellence and independent thinking.

If you're passionate about robotics, have strong programming skills (Python/C++, ROS2), and want to work on real systems that matter, explore opportunities or reach out directly.
